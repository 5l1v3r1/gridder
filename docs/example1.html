Example 1 Input

2           (number of dimensions)
1           (number of regions in axis x)
     0      (beginning coordinate of region 1, axis x)
    10      (end coordinate of region 1, axis x)
10          (number of divisions in region 1, axis x)
3           (type of spacing for region 1, axis x = logarithmic small to large)
1           (number of regions in axis y)
     0      (beginning coordinate of region 1, axis y)
    10      (end coordinate of region 1, axis y)
10          (number of divisions in region 1, axis y)
1           (type of spacing in region 1, axis y = equal)
1           (type of output = AVS format)

The last number in the list would be a 2 for Tracer3d format.
